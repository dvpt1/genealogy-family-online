Family genealogy Online

Applications

Preserve history, start with yourself!

The online family tree app is designed for online creation and editing of a relatives' database. Generating genealogical reports: family tree, family tree branch, family map, generational chart, and date calendar. Relatives and everyone involved in creating the shared family history can communicate with each other in real time and make changes to the database. Add photos and copies of documents.

Creating and Editing a Relatives Database
• The application allows you to create and edit a relatives database online.
• You can generate various genealogical reports: tree, branch, map, generational chart, and date calendar.
Communication and Interaction
• Relatives and other participants can communicate with each other in real time.
• This facilitates the collaborative creation and updating of a shared family history.
Making Amendments and Adding Materials
• Users can make amendments to the database.
• You can add photographs and copies of documents for a more complete view of your family history.

The online family tree app is an indispensable tool for those seeking to uncover the secrets of their family tree and create a complete picture of their family ties. The number of users is growing every day, thanks to the convenience and functionality offered by this app. Its ease of use allows even the most inexperienced users to build their own family trees with ease, attracting the attention of both the younger generation and older people.

Users can not only create various genealogical reports, such as family trees, branches, and maps, but also master the date calendar, which helps capture important events in family life. This functionality allows for the creation of a comprehensive and detailed picture of the family, significantly facilitating the work of researching family history.

The platform features real-time communication, allowing relatives to interact with each other, exchanging ideas, corrections, and memories. This creates a unique atmosphere of collaboration, where everyone can contribute to the creation of a shared history. The ability to add photographs and copies of documents helps preserve the memory of ancestors and increases the value of the database being created.

Therefore, the online family tree app not only helps recreate family history but also brings relatives closer together, allowing them to view their heritage from a new perspective. Each user, using this app, can become not only a researcher but also a keeper of family memories, creating unique and vibrant connections between generations.
